Dataset Of Dogbites - Pie Charts

In this Part of our website you can use our Pie Charts to visualize the data from our Dogbites dataset. this helps us understand the amount of dog bites across different areas of the city. This chart also some of the places we should be careful in since they have the highest percentages. The borough with the most Cases of dog bites is Queens and the lowest amount of dog bites is Staten Island All of the data is from NYC Open Data.

How to use: Here you can click on this drop down menu. This menu will give you a few options to choose from when it comes to visuals. you can choose a Pie chart , Bar Graph , or a donut chaped chart!
